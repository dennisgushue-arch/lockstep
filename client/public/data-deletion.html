Lockstep Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Lockstep account and associated personal data.

How to Request Deletion

Email support@lockstep.app with the subject line Data Deletion Request - Lockstep.

Please include:

  • Your Lockstep account email address
  • Any relevant user ID (if available)

Verification

To protect your account, we may ask you to verify your identity before we process a deletion request.

What We Delete

  • Account profile data
  • User-generated content (for example commitments, reflections, journal entries, and voice note records)
  • Connected integration metadata linked to your account where feasible

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited information where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, tax/accounting, or regulatory obligations.

Processing Timeline

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days (or sooner where required by law).

Contact

Deletion requests: support@lockstep.app
Privacy questions: privacy@lockstep.app

Legal pages: Privacy · Terms · Data Deletion

This policy is intended to satisfy Google Play account-deletion disclosure requirements for account-based apps.
