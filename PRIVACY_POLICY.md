# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 5, 2026

Lockstep ("Lockstep," "we," "our," or "us") provides a commitment accountability platform that helps users create and track commitments, optionally with financial stakes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our website, mobile app, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

### a) Information You Provide Directly

- Account details (such as email address)
- Commitment content (e.g., goals, reflections, journal text, notes)
- Voice note content and transcriptions (if you use voice features)
- Settings and preferences
- Customer support messages

### b) Information Collected Automatically

- Device/browser information
- Log and usage data (pages viewed, actions taken, timestamps)
- IP address and approximate location derived from IP
- Cookies and similar technologies

### c) Payment Information

If you use paid or stake-related features, payment processing may be handled by a third-party provider (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full payment card numbers. We may receive limited billing metadata (e.g., payment status, subscription state, transaction identifiers).

### d) Integrations and Connected Sources

If you choose to connect external sources (such as messaging, calendar, or other integrations), we may process data from those sources solely to provide platform functionality you enable.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide, operate, and improve the Services
- Authenticate users and manage accounts
- Analyze intent/commitment patterns and generate related prompts or recommendations
- Manage commitments, stakes, reflections, and history
- Process transactions and prevent fraud/abuse
- Communicate with you about service updates and support
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## 3. Legal Bases for Processing (Where Applicable)

Depending on your location, we rely on one or more legal bases:

- Performance of a contract (providing requested Services)
- Legitimate interests (improving security, product quality, fraud prevention)
- Consent (where required, such as certain optional features)
- Legal obligation (compliance with applicable laws)

## 4. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

- Service providers and infrastructure vendors (hosting, analytics, auth, database, email, payment processing)
- Integration partners you authorize
- Legal/regulatory authorities when required by law

We do **not** sell your personal information for money.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including to:

Retention periods may vary by data type and jurisdiction.
