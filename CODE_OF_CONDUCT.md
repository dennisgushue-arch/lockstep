# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Lockstep is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for all. We value the participation of every member and want everyone to feel respected and supported.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** - Treat all community members with courtesy and kindness
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and identities
- **Be collaborative** - Work together constructively toward common goals
- **Be professional** - Maintain high standards in communication and conduct
- **Be accountable** - Take responsibility for your actions and their impact

### Examples of Good Behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other members
- Helping others succeed and grow

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are **not tolerated**:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
- Discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, disability, nationality, or other protected characteristics
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics or choices
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Threats of violence or violent language
- Posting or displaying sexually explicit material
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking
- Disruption of discussions or events
- Publication of private information without consent
- Engaging in inflammatory debates unrelated to the project
- Spamming or promoting unrelated content

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it:

**Email:** [conduct@lockstep.app](mailto:conduct@lockstep.app)

Reports should include:

- Your contact information
- Description of the incident
- Date, time, and location
- Names of involved parties
- Any relevant links or screenshots
- Whether this is ongoing

**All reports will be handled confidentially.**

### Investigation

- Reports are reviewed within 5 business days
- We will investigate thoroughly and fairly
- All parties have the opportunity to respond
- We will keep you informed of the outcome

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

1. **First Violation:** Warning and request to modify behavior
2. **Second Violation:** Temporary suspension (1-7 days)
3. **Third Violation:** Permanent ban from the community

For severe violations (threats, harassment, harassment), immediate bans may be issued.

**Bans include:**

- Removal from GitHub organization
- Removal from community discussions
- Revocation of contributor access
- Public announcement of ban (optional, at moderator discretion)

## Community Guidelines by Context

### GitHub Issues & Pull Requests

- Stay on topic
- Use constructive language when providing feedback
- Link to relevant issues or discussions
- Avoid cross-posting or duplicate issues
- Don't use issues for support questions (use Discussions instead)

### Discussions & Forums

- Respect the topic of each discussion
- Search before creating duplicate discussions
- Help others when you can
- Don't promote unrelated products or services
- Keep conversations civil and productive

### External Communication

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Official Lockstep communication channels
- Community events and meetings
- When representing Lockstep publicly

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- ✅ All community members (contributors, maintainers, users)
- ✅ All official Lockstep spaces (GitHub, forums, events)
- ✅ Off-platform conduct that affects the community
