Play Console Data Safety Variant Selector (Lockstep)

Files
- PLAY_CONSOLE_DATA_SAFETY_STRICT_MINIMAL.txt
- PLAY_CONSOLE_DATA_SAFETY_CONSERVATIVE.txt
- PLAY_CONSOLE_DATA_SAFETY_ANSWER_SHEET.txt (balanced baseline)

When to use each

1) Strict / Minimal
Use PLAY_CONSOLE_DATA_SAFETY_STRICT_MINIMAL.txt only if engineering can prove:
- optional features are true opt-in,
- no hidden collection paths exist,
- all "Shared: NO" answers satisfy current Google Play definitions.

2) Conservative / Over-disclosure
Use PLAY_CONSOLE_DATA_SAFETY_CONSERVATIVE.txt when unsure.
This is generally safer for avoiding mismatch-based policy issues, but may disclose more than technically required.

3) Baseline (Balanced)
Use PLAY_CONSOLE_DATA_SAFETY_ANSWER_SHEET.txt for middle-ground interpretation based on currently documented behavior.

Final gate before submit
- Confirm policy URLs are live and public.
- Confirm Data Deletion URL is entered.
- Confirm policy text and Data Safety selections match exactly.
- Recheck after any SDK, permission, or feature-flag changes.
