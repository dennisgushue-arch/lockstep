# Privacy Policy for Lockstep

**Effective date:** 2026-03-26  
**Last updated:** 2026-03-26

Lockstep ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Lockstep mobile app and related services (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, we may collect:

- **Account information** (e.g., email address and authentication identifiers)
- **User content** (e.g., commitments, reflections, journal entries, settings, and preferences)
- **Voice note data** (audio recordings and transcriptions) when you choose to use voice features
- **Connected source data** from integrations you enable (e.g., Google Calendar metadata)
- **Payment metadata** for paid features or stake flows (e.g., transaction identifiers and payment status); full payment card details are handled by payment processors
- **Device and usage information** (e.g., app interactions, timestamps, device/browser characteristics, logs)

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide and maintain Lockstep core features
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Process commitments, reflections, and optional stake/credit activity
- Generate insights and recommendations, including AI-assisted analysis
- Improve reliability, performance, and abuse/fraud prevention
- Communicate important service and support updates
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Legal Bases (EEA/UK, if applicable)

Where required by law, we rely on one or more legal bases:

- Performance of a contract
- Legitimate interests
- Consent
- Legal obligation

## 4. Sharing of Information

We may share data with:

- **Infrastructure and platform providers** (e.g., hosting, authentication, database/storage)
- **Payment processors** (e.g., Stripe)
- **AI processing providers** used to transcribe/analyze voice notes you submit (e.g., OpenAI APIs)
- **Integration partners** you authorize (e.g., Google services for calendar sync)
- **Legal authorities** when required by law
- **Business transferees** in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

We do **not** sell personal information.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The Services may use third-party SDKs/services such as:

- Supabase (authentication, database, storage, serverless functions)
- Stripe (payments)
- OpenAI APIs (voice transcription/analysis features)
- Google APIs (when you connect Google Calendar)

Each provider has its own privacy policy and data practices.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. No system is 100% secure.

## 8. Children’s Privacy

Lockstep is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

- Delete your data
- Object to or restrict processing
- Request data portability
- Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent)
