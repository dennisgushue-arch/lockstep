# Google Play Legal & Compliance Checklist for Lockstep

Use this checklist before submitting to production.

## Required for Lockstep Release

- [ ] Public **Privacy Policy URL** (HTTPS, no login required)
- [ ] Public **Data Deletion URL** (HTTPS, no login required)
- [ ] Privacy policy reflects: account data, commitments/reflections, voice notes/transcriptions, payment metadata, and optional integrations
- [ ] Play Console **Data Safety** form fully completed and accurate
- [ ] Support email configured and monitored (`support@lockstep.app`)

## Lockstep-Specific Data Handling

- [ ] Account deletion request flow documented in-app and policy docs
- [ ] Deletion requests processed within stated timeline (30 days target)
- [ ] If no self-serve in-app deletion yet, support-channel fallback is clearly documented

## Recommended Legal Documents and Links

- [ ] Terms of Service / EULA URL
- [ ] Support/Contact page URL
- [ ] Copyright/Trademark notice (if applicable)
- [ ] AI advisory language included (suggestions are informational, not professional advice)
- [ ] Financial stake language included (credits/stakes are optional and not gambling)

## Special Cases (mark if applicable)

- [ ] Children/Families policy disclosures (Lockstep currently: not directed to children)
- [ ] Ads disclosure and ad SDK compliance (Lockstep currently: no ad SDK evidence found)
- [ ] Health data disclaimers/compliance
- [ ] Financial app disclosures/licensing statements
- [ ] Location background use justification
- [ ] AI-generated content disclosure (recommended for Lockstep)

## Final Validation Pass

- [ ] Policy links open on mobile + desktop
- [ ] Policy dates updated
- [ ] Company legal name and contact details consistent across docs
- [ ] Data categories in policy match Data Safety answers exactly
- [ ] Deletion request inbox monitored and owner assigned

---

## Suggested Hosting Options

- Your company website (preferred)
- GitHub Pages
- Static hosting (Cloudflare Pages, Netlify, Firebase Hosting)

Once hosted, place URLs in:

1. Play Console > App content > Privacy Policy  
2. Play Console > Data safety (and account deletion fields, if shown)  
3. Store listing (Terms URL recommended)
