Lockstep Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Lockstep ("we", "our", "us") helps users create and track commitments. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your options.

Information We Collect

  • Account data (email, authentication identifiers).
  • User-generated content such as commitments, reflections, and voice notes.
  • Usage and device metadata needed for security, debugging, and feature reliability.
  • Payment metadata when purchases are enabled (handled by Stripe).

How We Use Information

  • Provide core product features and account access.
  • Generate commitment insights and recommendations.
  • Improve product quality, reliability, and abuse prevention.
  • Process transactions when paid features are used.

Voice Notes

Voice recordings are uploaded only when you explicitly choose to record and submit them. Microphone access is used strictly for in-app recording functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with infrastructure providers (for example hosting, authentication, and payment processors) strictly to operate the service.

Data Retention

We retain data while your account remains active, or as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can request account deletion and data removal by contacting support.
  • You can stop microphone access at any time from system settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@lockstep.app
