Lockstep Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This page explains how to request account deletion and what data is deleted or retained after your request.

How to Request Deletion

  • In-app (recommended): go to Settings and select the delete account option, if available.
  • Email request: send your request from your account email address to support@lockstep.app with subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Verification

For your security, we may require account verification before processing deletion requests.

What We Delete

  • Profile/account information linked to your user account.
  • Commitments, reflections, and related app content, subject to legal and operational limits.
  • Associated app-level preferences and identifiers used for normal service operation.

What We May Retain

  • Records required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, tax/accounting, and dispute resolution.
  • Limited payment/transaction records maintained by us or payment processors as required by law.
  • Security logs retained for a limited period where reasonably necessary.

Processing Timeline

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days, subject to legal exceptions.

Questions

Contact us at support@lockstep.app for help with deletion requests.
